Hi there! Welcome to My Page

I’m Elvina Sindartha, a Melbourne-based digital media enthusiast and Interior Design graduate with a love for the simple things in life—like enjoying a fresh cup of coffee in one of Melbourne’s many charming cafés.

Living in Melbourne for over ten years has given me a deep appreciation for the city’s café culture, with countless spots to enjoy and discover. Whether I’m indulging in my favorite matcha or exploring new corners of the city, my love for food and design is always at the forefront. Currently, as I pursue my Master’s in Digital Media, I’m dedicated to transforming my creative ideas into tangible projects.

I’ve been on a café-hopping adventure around Melbourne lately, and I thought I’d share some of my favorite finds with you. From the perfect flat white to mouthwatering pastries, these places have won me over, and I think you’ll love them too!
